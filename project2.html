HPR Tickets

The HPR Ticket bot was something that I am really proud to have completed. This bot worked flawlessly and never had any issues. This bot made both user and moderation lives easier. Users would have their issues sorted so much quicker due to the bots capabilities of asking user specific questions in order to resolve their issue that much quicker. This bot also made moderation life so much easier due to them being able to quickly resolve issues with all of the information they needed to know, already given to them thanks to HPR Bot.


The code below is only snippets of code.

Tihs bot functioned using ButtonBuilder which allowed the user to click the button which opened the Modal Builder

Users could enter the information asked for them in the relevant text boxes making moderators life easier as they had all information they needed without having to ask the user after the ticket was made

Here is what the ticket looked like once it was open.

Once the moderator had dealt with the ticket, they could press the Close Ticket button which would open up a modal which they could optionally enter the reason for closing, once they entered the reason, it would send a DM to the ticket creator letting them know the ticket had been closed and the reason why it was closed.
